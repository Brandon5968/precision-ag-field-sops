### Proper shutdown vs sleep

Sleep means the screen goes dark but the device is still on. It wakes instantly.
Shutdown means the device is fully off and takes longer to start again.

`[CONFIRM: whether to sleep or shut down at the end of a day, per depot policy.]`

### Force restart when frozen

If the screen is stuck and nothing responds, a force restart almost always fixes it.

- **Zebra ET45:** press and hold the power button for at least **8 seconds**, until
  the Zebra or Android logo appears, then let go.
- **Getac laptop:** press and hold the power button for about **10 seconds** until it
  switches off, then press it once to turn it back on. `[CONFIRM: exact Getac model and any model-specific reset step.]`

!!! warning "Do this before calling for help"
    A force restart fixes most frozen screens. It does not delete your work.

## Touching and tapping

The ET45 has a touchscreen.

- **Tap** once to open or select something.
- **Long-press** (hold your finger down for a second) to bring up extra options.
- **Swipe** (slide your finger across the screen) to scroll or move between screens.

- **Getac (Windows):** press the **Windows key** on the keyboard. It looks like a flag,

## You cannot break it by tapping

The most common worry for people new to these devices is breaking something by
accident. Normal use does not cause damage. Tapping, opening apps, and taking photos
are all safe. If something looks wrong, close the app and start again.
