# :material-wifi: Staying connected

How to get online in the depot, in the field, and what to do when there is no signal.

!!! info "AT A GLANCE"
    In the depot, use Wi-Fi. In the field, use a mobile hotspot. No signal is normal,
    and the field apps are built to work offline.

## Connecting to Wi-Fi

1. Tap or click the Wi-Fi icon in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
2. Select the depot network. `[CONFIRM: depot Wi-Fi network name.]`

## Working with no signal

No signal in the paddock is normal. The survey work on the Getac runs offline, and data
syncs once you are back in range.
