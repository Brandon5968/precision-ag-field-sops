# :material-wrench: When things go wrong

Stay calm. Work through this page top to bottom. Most problems have a simple fix.

!!! info "AT A GLANCE"
    Try the fix here first. If it does not work, call for help. You will not make it
    worse by following these steps.

## The screen is frozen

Nothing happens when you tap or click.

1. Wait 30 seconds. Sometimes the device is just busy.
2. Hold the power button until the screen goes off. **ET45:** about 8 seconds.
   **Getac:** about 10 seconds.
3. Wait 10 seconds, then press the power button once to start it again.
4. If it does not start, the battery may be flat. Plug it in and wait two minutes
   before trying again.

## The device will not turn on

1. Plug it in to charge.
2. Wait two minutes.
3. Press the power button.
4. If still nothing, call for help. `[CONFIRM: who to call for a dead device.]`

## T3RRA will not open

1. Check the taskbar at the bottom of the screen. T3RRA may already be open.
2. If not, open it again from the desktop shortcut.
3. If it still will not open, restart the Getac and try once more.

## I do not know what an error message means

1. Write down or photograph the exact message, word for word.
2. Search that exact wording in the search bar at the top of this site.
3. If nothing comes up, call for help. `[CONFIRM: support contact.]`
