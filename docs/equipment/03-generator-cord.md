# :material-engine: Generator

Generator faults in the field — starting problems, no output, and the pull-cord.

!!! warning "DRAFT — pending sign-off"
    Every card is `[CONFIRM]`. The pull-cord card involves spring tension — do **not**
    treat the placeholder steps as a procedure.

!!! info "AT A GLANCE"
    Don't force a generator. Fuel, oil and the recoil starter cover most faults. The
    **pull-cord recoil spring is under tension** — treat any recoil work as STOP & CALL
    until confirmed otherwise.
