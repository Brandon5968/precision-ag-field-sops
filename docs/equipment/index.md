# Equipment & Repairs

*Keeping the rig running in the field — and knowing when to bring it home.*

!!! warning "DRAFT — pending sign-off"
    This manual is a working draft. Every procedure is marked `[CONFIRM]` until it is
    verified in the review meeting by the people who do the work. Do not rely on it in
    the field yet.

This book covers the mechanical kit a field tech depends on — the Polaris, the generator,
power and cabling, and the survey rig — and the **one decision that matters when something
breaks: can I fix this here, do I take it home, or do I stop and call?**

!!! info "AT A GLANCE"
    Every repair is tagged **FIELD FIX**, **HOME FIX**, or **STOP & CALL**. If the part
    or tool isn't in your kit (Chapter 01), it's a **home fix**. Never start a mechanical
    job without completing the SWMS.
