# :material-wrench: Troubleshooting

Every fault at a glance: what you see, whether it's a field/home/stop job, and where to
go next. Full steps are in the chapter for each piece of gear.

    or stop and call. **If the part isn't in your [kit](01-field-kit.md), it's a home fix.**
